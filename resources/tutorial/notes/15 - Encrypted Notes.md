# 15 - Encrypted Notes

El Baton doesn't support encrypted notes yet, but if you really need this you could make an encrypted image on your computer and put a data directory in there. 

This way the third-party program will take care of the encryption.

If there's a big demand for this perhaps support for encrypted notes can be added to El Baton itself, [let us know](https://github.com/samhaswon/el-baton/issues) if you need this.
